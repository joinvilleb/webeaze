Rebrand to WebEaze


Date: January 1, 2025

For our early clients, we've served you under Billy Joinville Web Services. We're immensely grateful for you because we couldn't be where we are today without you. Truly. From when we founded in October 2024, we've grown and now have a total of 26 clients. We've never made a full transition article about our rebranding decision but beginning January 1, 2025, we're fully rebranding to a new name, WebEaze. This name reflects our mission to make websites effortless yet elegant for our clients. We want to make it easy for you to manage your website, get support, and grow together.

We know this is a big change, but we believe this was the right decision for us. Over the next few months and throughout the remainder of 2025, we're introducing new features and streamlining our operations to make web design easier for you. We want to be your go-to web design company, and we believe this is the first step in that direction. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
