Refund Policy

All sales are final. We fix issues fast.

Because our services start immediately, refunds aren't offered. But we stand by our work with clear remedies for service issues and prompt corrections for billing errors.

Effective: Oct 1, 2024 Subscriptions: monthly & yearly Help Center & Live Support
Summary

What this policy means

Final Sale

Setup fees, monthly or annual plans, and add-ons are non-refundable due to immediate time and expertise involved.

We Remedy Issues

Within your plan scope, we provide revisions, bug fixes, and status updates to resolve problems quickly.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your plan anytime through your Client Portal. Services end immediately upon cancellation and no further charges occur. There are no refunds for unused time.

Details

How we handle common situations

Service Issues
  • Contact us promptly with a short description (screenshots help).
  • We confirm scope and timeline; then deliver fixes or revisions.
  • If outside plan scope, we'll propose options before work.
Accidental or Duplicate Charges
  • Share the charge date, amount, and last 4 digits of the card.
  • Upon verification, we issue a reversal or account credit.
Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback after services are delivered is misuse of the payment process. We provide timelines, approvals, and deliverables as evidence. Proven misuse may lead to recovery of costs and suspension of service. We always prefer a direct, fair resolution first.

Your Legal Rights

Nothing here limits non-waivable consumer rights under applicable law. Where required, we honor mandatory cooling-off or refund rights.
