Scope

This policy applies to WebEaze subscriptions and one time setup fees. It covers service issues, billing errors, cancellations, and chargebacks.

Final sale

Payments are final. This includes setup fees, monthly subscriptions, yearly subscriptions, and approved add ons. Work begins immediately after payment and time is reserved on our schedule.

Service issues

If something is not right, contact us and include a short description. Screenshots help. We will:

  • Confirm the issue and the goal
  • Give a plan with a clear timeline
  • Deliver fixes or revisions within your plan scope
Out of scope  If a request is outside your plan we will outline options and costs before work starts.

Billing errors

If you see an accidental or duplicate charge, email support@webeaze.io with the charge date, amount, and last four digits of the card. After verification we issue a reversal or account credit.

Cancellation

You can cancel any time in the Client Portal. Your subscription stops at cancellation and there are no more charges. There are no refunds for unused time.

Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback after services are delivered is misuse of the payment process. We provide project approvals, timelines, and deliverables as evidence. Proven misuse can lead to recovery of costs and suspension of service. We always prefer a direct, fair resolution first.

Your rights

Nothing in this policy limits non waivable consumer rights under applicable law. If your local law requires a cooling off period or refund in specific cases, we will comply.
