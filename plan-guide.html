Essential

Up to 3 pages

Ideal for startups and solo founders who need a clean, professional site with dependable support. All without a big upfront spend.

What you get

  • ✓Custom Website Design & Pages: up to 3 pages tailored to your audience.
  • ✓Domain & Hosting Setup: we connect your domain and configure secure hosting.
  • ✓Mobile Optimization: fully responsive across devices.
  • ✓SEO Tools: foundational SEO for visibility.
  • ✓Unlimited Maintenance: submit updates via the Website Request form.

What’s not included

  • — Domain/hosting coverage (you pay vendor fees directly)
  • — Live chat or Zoom support
  • — Performance reports
  • — Annual website tune‑up
  • — Google Business Profile (GBP) management
Best for: New businesses with a focused 1–3 page presence that can grow later.
Upgrade cues
  • Need 4+ pages or landing pages for ads?
  • Prefer Zoom/Live Chat support over form‑only?
  • Want monthly performance insights?
  • Ready for GBP setup to improve local search?

Growth Popular

Up to 6 pages (+ legacy sites okay)

Great for scaling businesses that want more pages, more tools, and more responsive support.

What you get

  • ✓Custom Website Design & Pages: up to 6 pages with flexible layouts and branding.
  • ✓Domain & Hosting Setup: full setup including DNS and SSL guidance.
  • ✓Mobile Optimization: every page is responsive.
  • ✓Domain/Hosting Coverage: up to $50 toward vendor fees.
  • ✓Unlimited Maintenance & Support: via Email, Zoom, and Live Chat.
  • ✓Performance Reports: monthly reports when there’s room to improve.
  • ✓Works with Existing Sites: we’ll include all current pages even if you exceed six.
  • ✓GBP Setup & Optimization: we’ll create or optimize your Google Business Profile (Maps/local SEO, info, keywords, photos).
Best for: Service providers, educators, and teams growing their presence with ongoing collaboration.
When to consider Elite
  • Need unlimited pages or frequent landing pages?
  • Want full domain/hosting coverage?
  • Need priority response and faster turnarounds?
  • Want advanced reports (analytics + keyword tracking)?
  • Prefer ongoing GBP management beyond setup?

Unlimited pages • Priority

For ambitious brands that want full‑scale support, unlimited pages, and done‑for‑you growth tools.

  • ✓Mobile Optimization: mobile‑first layouts throughout.
  • ✓SEO Tools (Enhanced): visibility upgrades, structured data, and keyword focus.
  • ✓Domain/Hosting Coverage: we fully cover your vendor fees.
  • ✓Unlimited Maintenance & Support: priority Email, Zoom, and Live Chat with expedited response.
