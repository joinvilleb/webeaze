What is WebEaze? ▼

WebEaze is small web design company that offers affordable, professional websites for individuals and organizations. We provide a range of services to help you get online quickly and easily, including website design, hosting, and maintenance. Our goal is to make it effortless for you to create a website that looks great and works well, without the hassle of doing it yourself.

Do you have any samples? ▼

Yes! We have a few samples under our 'Samples are important' section. You can also email us and we'll provide a full list of samples for you to look at.

What if I have an existing website? ▼

We offer services for existing websites, including updates, redesigns, and troubleshooting. Our Growth and Elite packages are designed to help you grow your online presence and reach your goals.

When can you finish my website? ▼

For new websites, we aim to complete your website within 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of the project. For existing websites, we'll work with you to find a timeline that works. We'll keep you updated on our progress and let you know when your website is ready to go live.

How much support do I get? ▼

We offer 12 months of support with any package you purchase. This includes updates, maintenance, and troubleshooting. We're here to help, so you can reach out to us anytime you need assistance.

Are there any other costs after purchasing the package? ▼

No! We're transparent about our pricing and services, so you know exactly what you're getting upfront. Any external service fees (e.g., domain registration, hosting) will be communicated to you before any purchase is made.
