What is WebEaze? ▼

WebEaze is a niche-focused web design company that creates affordable, professional websites for small businesses—especially tutors, trade professionals, and travel experts. From design and hosting to updates and support, we make it easy to launch and grow your online presence without the tech overwhelm.

Do you have any samples? ▼

Absolutely. You'll find examples of tutoring, travel, and trade websites we've built in the “Samples are important” section below. Need something specific? Contact us and we'll send you tailored examples for your industry.

What if I have an existing website? ▼

We love helping businesses level up. Whether you're a tutoring center needing a redesign or a travel business adding booking features—we offer site audits, updates, and full redesigns. Our Growth and Elite packages are perfect for established businesses ready to grow.

When can you finish my website? ▼

Most sites are ready within 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity. A simple tutoring site or portfolio may take just a few days. For larger projects, like travel itineraries or multi-page service sites, we'll give you a clear timeline during your consultation.

How much support do I get? ▼

Every WebEaze package includes ongoing support for 12 months—or as long as your subscription is active. That means unlimited updates, security checks, and help whenever you need it. Whether it's fixing a broken link or adding new class offerings, we've got your back.

Are there any other costs after purchasing the package? ▼

No surprise fees. We're upfront about everything. If you need extras like a custom domain, premium hosting, or add-ons (like booking tools), we'll walk you through the costs before anything is purchased.

What platforms do you build on? ▼

We build fully custom websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to give you complete flexibility—no bulky templates or hidden platform fees. For clients who need blog functionality, booking systems, or e-commerce, we also work with platforms like WordPress, Shopify, and Squarespace (based on your needs). During your consultation, we'll recommend the best setup for your business.
